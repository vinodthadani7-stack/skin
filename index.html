Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We have answers. Here are the most common things patients ask us.

How many sessions are needed for laser hair removal?
+
Typically 6–8 sessions are required for permanent hair reduction. Sessions are spaced 4–6 weeks apart depending on the treatment area and hair growth cycle.
Is laser hair removal safe for Indian skin?
+
Yes! At SkinVilla, we use advanced diode laser technology calibrated specifically for Indian skin tones, making it safe and effective for all skin types including darker complexions.
How long does an acne treatment plan take?
+
Active acne can typically be controlled in 4–8 weeks with the right combination of topical and oral medications. Scar reduction may take 3–6 months depending on severity.
What is the consultation fee?
+
Consultation fees are very affordable. Please call us or book online to know the current fee structure. Treatment costs vary based on the procedure and number of sessions.
Does PRP hair treatment actually work?
+
PRP therapy has strong clinical evidence supporting hair regrowth. Most patients see noticeable results after 3–6 sessions, especially for early to moderate hair loss.
How do I book an appointment?
+
You can book via our website form (it goes directly to WhatsApp), call us, or walk in during clinic hours (Mon–Sat, 10am–8pm). We confirm every booking personally.
Where exactly is the clinic in Jalgaon?
+
We are at 16, Ring Road, beside New Life Hospital, Hareshwar Nagar, Tagore Nagar, Jalgaon 425001. Easily accessible with ample parking.
Are pigmentation treatments permanent?
+
With proper sun protection and maintenance, results from our pigmentation treatments are long-lasting. We combine Q-switch laser, chemical peels, and medical creams.

About the Doctor

Dedicated to Your Skin's Health

Dr. Nikita is a highly qualified dermatologist and certified hair transplant surgeon with a deep passion for transforming lives through advanced skincare. After completing her MBBS and DDV (Diploma in Dermatology, Venereology) from Mumbai — one of India's premier medical hubs — she returned to Jalgaon with a mission: to provide high-quality, accessible skin and hair care to the people of North Maharashtra.

With over 8 years of clinical experience, Dr. Nikita has successfully treated thousands of patients suffering from acne, hair loss, pigmentation, and various skin disorders. Her approach combines evidence-based medicine with the latest laser and aesthetic dermatology technologies.

Beyond clinical expertise, Dr. Nikita's warm, patient-first philosophy sets SkinVilla apart. She takes time to understand each patient's unique concerns and designs personalized treatment protocols that deliver real, visible results.
